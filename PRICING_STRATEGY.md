# PathAI Pricing Strategy

## Recommended Pricing: $9-19/month

Based on the critique analysis and implemented features, PathAI provides significantly more value than ChatGPT + Notion DIY approach.

---

## Tier Comparison

### Free Tier (Limited)
**Price**: $0/month

**Features**:
- ✅ Basic task management (up to 20 tasks)
- ✅ 1 active goal
- ✅ Manual task creation
- ✅ Basic daily planner
- ❌ No AI task generation
- ❌ No performance insights
- ❌ No adaptive coaching
- ❌ No proactive notifications
- ❌ No feasibility validation

**Target**: Students trying the app, casual users

---

### Pro Tier (Recommended)
**Price**: $12/month ($120/year with 17% discount)

**Features**:
- ✅ **Unlimited tasks and goals**
- ✅ **AI-powered task generation** with web research
- ✅ **Feasibility validation** (prevents wasted time on impossible goals)
- ✅ **Performance insights** (30-day pattern analysis)
- ✅ **Adaptive coaching** (Plan-B interventions when falling behind)
- ✅ **Intelligent task splitting** (breaks down overwhelming tasks)
- ✅ **Proactive push notifications** (morning motivation, evening check-ins)
- ✅ **Context-aware Daily Pulse** (personalized based on your patterns)
- ✅ **Priority support**
- ✅ **Weekly reports** (shareable progress summaries)

**Target**: Serious goal achievers (university applications, career switches, fitness goals)

---

**Features**:

## Why $12/month is the Sweet Spot

### 1. **Cost Structure**
- OpenAI API costs: ~$2-3/user/month (heavy usage)
- Expo push notifications: ~$0.50/user/month
- Infrastructure (AWS): ~$1/user/month
- **Gross margin**: ~$7.50/user/month (62%)

### 2. **Market Positioning**
- **Notion**: $10/month (productivity)
- **Todoist Premium**: $4/month (basic tasks)
- **Motion**: $34/month (AI calendar)
- **ChatGPT Plus**: $20/month (general AI)

**PathAI at $12**: Premium features without enterprise pricing.

### 3. **Psychological Pricing**
- $9.99 feels "cheap" but undervalues the product
- $19.99 triggers "is it worth $20?" hesitation
- **$11.99** hits sweet spot: Premium but affordable

### 4. **Target Market Validation**
- University applicants: Saving $15,000 in admissions consultant fees
- Career switchers: Saving months of trial & error
- Fitness goals: Saving $100+/month in personal trainer fees

**Conclusion**: $12/month is 0.08% of what users save → massive value.
